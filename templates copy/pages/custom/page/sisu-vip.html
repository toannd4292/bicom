SISU VIP Membership FAQs

Where do I go if I have questions?

For any questions regarding the VIP program, please contact customerservice@sisuguard.com or chat with us!

Can I combine discounts?

You cannot combine additional discounts or promotions with your SISU VIP, but you will always get 20% off all in-store items while an active member regardless of how many purchases you make.

Will the SISU VIP membership fee change?

The membership fee is subject to change at any point, but if you’re an existing member then you won’t be affected by this. Existing members lock in their membership fee once they first sign up, and as is stated above your discount increases by 5% for each year you remain a member (*up to 4 years).

When can I apply my SISU VIP discount?

Right away! Add the SISU VIP membership to your cart and we’ll apply the 20% off discount to anything in your cart at checkout!

Can I use my SISU VIP membership in stores?

The SISU VIP membership can only be used on the SISU website, but our site offers every product you might find in stores - and more.

Is my SISU VIP membership refundable?

The SISU VIP membership fee is non-refundable.

Will my membership automatically renew?

The SISU VIP membership will automatically renew 12 months after the initial payment. We’ll send you a reminder via email 30-days before the renewal date in case you don’t want to continue your SISU VIP membership.

What happens if I cancel my membership?

Memberships are valid for 12 months after purchase. If you cancel your membership the day after you buy it, you’ll still have access to all the exclusive benefits for 364 more days!

Where is the VIP membership available?

The SISU VIP membership is only available in North America - for now!
